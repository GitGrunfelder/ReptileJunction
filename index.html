Welcome to Reptile Junction

We are Reptile Junction, serving the Upstate with all the scaly (or not so scaly) critters you have ever wanted. Though we are based in the Carolinas, you can find us traveling to repticons and other conventions all over the east coast.

You can follow us on social media to see what adventures we have ahead and to keep up with the current critters! We also provide care sheets, tips, and recommendations for the animals we sell here.

We breed and sell only crested geckos and panther chameleons at the moment, with plans to expand our offerings in the near future. We also offer any supplies a reptile owner may need, ranging from heat lamps and enclosures to crickets and mealworms. We hope to fulfill any of your reptile needs!

Our Philosophy

Everyone here at Reptile Junction believes that communication between staff and customers is key to ensuring an outstanding user experience. We strive to answer any and all questions users may have about our products and how to best take care of any newly purchased pets. Quality is at the forefront of our hearts when it comes to reptiles and reptile products! If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, do not hesitate to reach out.

Thank you for checking out our page! We hope you found everything you were looking for, and if not, please reach out to us! We would love your feedback. Also a big thanks for all your support.
